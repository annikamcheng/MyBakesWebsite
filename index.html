Mochi Recipe:

Ingredients:

250ml Milk

170g Sweet Rice Flour

35g Sugar

1tsp Vanilla Extract

1/8tsp Salt

1-2tsp Matcha (Optional)

Cornstarch (So dough isn't sticky)

Instructions:

1. Sift flour, sugar, and salt together.

2. Pour in the milk and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth and no lumps.

3. Microwave the dough in a heat-safe bowl at 2 minute intervals until dough is cooked.

4. Wait for the dough to cool. Beat it with a rolling pin or with your hands.

5. Shape the dough and put it on a cutting dough that has cornstarch on it. Fill with whatever you like! Red bean is a common filling but sesame paste is also delicious!

Bread Recipe:

Ingredients:

200g Bread Flour

20g Sugar

3g Salt

5g Non-Fat Dry Milk Powder

100g Water

3g Dry Yeast

30g Beaten Egg

25g Unsalted Butter

45g Unsalted Butter (For Topping)

1 tbsp Beaten Egg (For Brushing on Bread)

Instructions:

1. Combine the dry ingredients and mix together with a whisk.

2. Combine water, dry yeast, egg, and then add it to the dry ingredients and mix.

3. Knead the dough in the bowl until no more residue is left and transfer to a cutting board.

4. Add butter and knead more until the dough is smooth.

5. Make it into a ball, then leave it in a warm place until it gets twice as big.

6. Take the dough and divide it into 16 pieces and make them round.

7. Cover them with a wet cloth and leave them for 15 mins.

8. After resting, deflate with your hand, round the ball into circles and firmly close the seam.

9. Put them in a square pan and cover them with a wet cloth. Leave them in a warm place until they get twice as big.

10. Coat the dough with beaten egg and put butter on top.

11. Bake it in the preheated oven (410°F) for about 14 minutes.

12. Remove the bread from the pan and place it on a wire rack to cool.

Matcha Cookies:

Ingredients:

120g Unsalted Butter

2g Salt

90g Sugar

30g Egg

2g Vanilla Extract

220g All-Purpose Flour

32g Cornstarch

2g Baking Powder

5g Matcha Powder

Instructions:

1. Sift flour, salt, sugar, cornstarch, baking powder, and matcha together.

2. Cream together the sugar and butter until smooth.

3. Beat in the egg and vanilla until mixed together.

4. Pour in half the flour mixture into the mixed egg and butter. Fold the batter and pour in the other half.

5. Roll out the dough into a 1cm thick flat dough board.

6. Refrigerate dough for 30 minutes.

7. Cut out cookies into cute shapes and bake at 338°F for ten minutes.
